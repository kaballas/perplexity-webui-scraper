FR-7	Essential	The solution must support online organisational charts with visibility across vacant, filled and shared positions.
FR-8	Essential	The solution must support the ongoing management of a hierarchical organisational structure across past, current and future organisational modelling scenarios.

FR-12	Essential	The solution must support the changing of jobs with a date effective with the option to change / update any of the data attributes stored against the job.

FR-14	Essential	The solution must support the automatic inheriting of data attributes stored against jobs through to the creation of new positions.

FR-16	Essential	The solution must support the loading and storage of role descriptions against positions.
FR-17	Essential	The solution must provide warnings when a request is submitted to create a new position would result in the approved FTE cap for that organisational unit to be exceeded.
FR-18	Essential	The solution must support the changing of positions with a date effective with the option to change / update any of the data attributes stored against the position.
FR-19	Essential	The solution must support the abolishing of positions with a date effective.
FR-20	Essential	The solution must support concurrent employment scenarios across the Department, including concurrent employment in the same award and concurrent employment across awards.
FR-21	Essential	The solution must support the bulk creation of positions to manage annual school allocations FTE and funding source with the option to split out to reflect school based direct and indirect reporting lines.
FR-22	Essential	The solution must support the bulk creation and update of organisational units and positions to manage Machinery of Government changes or large organisational restructures.
FR-23	Essential	The solution must allow line managers to create and edit positions based on allowable permissions (e.g. schools to create self-funded positions), which automatically updates the schools establishment without the need for additional workflow approvals.
FR-24	Essential	The solution must support Headcount and FTE management for Corporate positions at both a position level and positions within an organisational unit.
FR-25	Essential	The solution must support Resource and FTE management for School positions at both a position level and positions within an organisational unit.
FR-26	Essential	The solution must support a maximum allowed allocation of FTE against positions at multiple levels of the org structure (e.g. unit, branch or division).
FR-27	Essential	The solution must support vacancy management, including vacancy management reporting, across 1:1 position management, 1:Many position management and a hybrid model of both 1:1 and 1:Many establishment management model.
FR-28	Essential	The solution must support the workflow of create position and change position to internal teams (JADE – Job Analysis Design & Establishment, Finance) for review and or approval based on pre-defined parameters e.g. position is a permanent position or a Senior Executive Service (SES) position.
FR-29	Essential	The solution must support the creation and ongoing management of positions for volunteers, contingent / contractor employees and casuals in a talent pool via 1:Many position management.
FR-30	Essential	The solution must support automatic occupancy management from employee movements including concurrent employment.
FR-31	Essential	The solution must allow an employee to occupy multiple positions numbers concurrently and accurately reflect the occupied concurrent positions against the Department’s establishment management and organisational structure.
FR-32	Essential	The solution must support position based allowances that are automatically included in payroll calculations for any employee assigned to that position.
FR-33	Essential	The solution must support the automatic inheriting of data attributes stored against positions through to the creation of new hires, with the option to override at the employee level.
FR-34	Essential	The solution must support the creation of locations / centres with a date effective and a range of data attributes e.g. Location ID, Location Name, Location Address, Location Contact Details and Longitude and Latitude details.
FR-35	Essential	The solution must support the changing of locations / centres with a date effective with the option to change / update any of the data attributes stored against the location / centre.
FR-36	Essential	The solution must support the abolishing of locations / centres with a date effective.
FR-37	Essential	The solution must support the recording of an employee’s work site location and their physical location e.g. work site location is a remote location and their physical location for certain days of the week is a personal residence or virtual academy, which has pay outcome implications e.g. no entitlement location based allowances if working from a personal residence that is outside of the remote location.
FR-38	Essential	The solution must support organisation design reporting including vacancy management reporting, occupancy management reporting, headcount and FTE reporting, organisational structure reporting and establishment management reporting with a consolidated view across organisational units, jobs, positions, locations, concurrent employment and key employee information.

## Checklist of Key Areas to Address

• **Integration Architecture**: Analyze CIS integration points and data synchronization mechanisms for organizational units
• **Security & Access Control**: Define role-based permissions for position creation, modification, and organizational structure management
• **Data Governance**: Address master data management for jobs, positions, and organizational hierarchies across time dimensions
• **Workflow & Approval**: Map approval processes for position creation and changes, particularly for JADE/Finance workflows
• **Concurrent Employment**: Design handling for complex employment scenarios and multi-position assignments
• **Reporting & Analytics**: Ensure comprehensive reporting capabilities for establishment management and organizational analytics

## Comprehensive SAP SuccessFactors Solution Analysis

### Integration Points Analysis

The requirements indicate a critical dependency on **Centre Information System (CIS) integration** (FR-4) that requires careful architectural consideration. The integration must support real-time synchronization of organizational units while maintaining data integrity across date-effective changes. 

**Key Integration Considerations:**
- **Master Data Synchronization**: Implement IDOC or OData-based integration patterns to ensure organizational unit changes in CIS propagate to SuccessFactors Employee Central
- **Change Detection**: Establish timestamp-based delta mechanisms to capture organizational changes without full data refreshes
- **Error Handling**: Design robust error handling for integration failures, including rollback capabilities for organizational structure changes
- **Data Mapping**: Map CIS organizational attributes to SuccessFactors foundation objects (FO) including departments, cost centers, and locations

The **bulk creation requirements** (FR-21, FR-22) suggest need for integration with external funding allocation systems, particularly for annual school FTE allocations and Machinery of Government changes.

### Security Considerations

**Role-Based Access Control** is fundamental given the decentralized nature of position management:
- **Line Manager Permissions**: Implement position management roles that allow school-based managers to create self-funded positions without workflow escalation
- **Data Security**: Apply field-level security for sensitive information like HR/Finance delegations, position-based allowances, and salary information
- **Hierarchical Security**: Ensure managers can only view and modify organizational units within their reporting chain
- **Audit Trail**: Maintain comprehensive audit logs for all organizational structure changes, particularly for compliance with establishment management requirements

**Workflow Security** must address the multi-team approval process involving JADE and Finance teams, requiring secure role assignments and escalation procedures for permanent and SES positions.

### Overall Design Gaps

**Time-Effective Data Management**: While requirements mention date-effective changes (FR-2, FR-3, FR-12), the design lacks clarity on:
- **Historical Data Retention**: How past organizational structures are maintained for reporting and compliance
- **Future-Dating Scenarios**: Management of organizational changes with future effective dates and their impact on planning
- **Version Control**: Handling of concurrent organizational models and their visibility across the enterprise

**Concurrent Employment Architecture**: FR-20 and FR-31 indicate complex multi-position scenarios that require:
- **Position Assignment Logic**: Clear rules for how employees can occupy multiple positions simultaneously
- **FTE Calculation**: Accurate calculation of total FTE when employees hold multiple positions
- **Payroll Integration**: Coordination with payroll systems for concurrent employment scenarios

**Establishment Management**: The solution lacks clarity on:
- **FTE Cap Enforcement**: How maximum FTE allocations (FR-26) are calculated and enforced across organizational hierarchies
- **Budget Integration**: Connection between position creation and budget/funding approval processes
- **Capacity Planning**: Integration with workforce planning tools for future organizational needs

**Reporting Architecture**: While FR-38 mentions comprehensive reporting, the underlying data model for:
- **Real-time Analytics**: How organizational charts and vacancy reports update with concurrent changes
- **Cross-Module Integration**: Connection between organizational management and other SuccessFactors modules
- **External Reporting**: Integration with business intelligence tools for executive reporting

### Technical Architecture Recommendations

**Foundation Objects Configuration**: Design custom MDF (Manageable Data Fields) objects for:
- Custom organizational attributes (Transfer Rating, Identified Position)
- Location-based allowances and work site tracking
- Job family and function hierarchies with ANZSCO mappings

**Workflow Engine**: Implement SuccessFactors Workflow Management for:
- Position approval processes with conditional routing
- Automatic FTE cap validation and warning generation
- Integration with external approval systems

**Integration Framework**: Leverage SAP Cloud Platform Integration for:
- CIS data synchronization
- Payroll system connectivity
- Third-party location and mapping services

# WRICEF Analysis for Queensland Department of Education SAP SuccessFactors Implementation

## **W - Workflows**

### Current State
- Position approval workflows for permanent and SES positions require JADE/Finance team approval
- Self-funded position creation by line managers without workflow escalation
- Multi-level approval processes for organizational changes

### Requirements
- **WF-001**: Position Creation and Approval Workflow - Support conditional routing based on position type (permanent/temporary), funding source (self-funded vs budget-funded), and level (SES vs non-SES)
- **WF-002**: Organizational Structure Change Workflow - Manage approval processes for department/division changes with stakeholder notifications
- **WF-003**: Concurrent Employment Workflow - Handle multiple position assignments for single employees with FTE validation
- **WF-004**: Bulk Position Creation Workflow - Support annual FTE allocation processes and Machinery of Government changes

### Technical Considerations
- SuccessFactors Workflow Management engine configuration
- Integration with external approval systems (JADE/Finance)
- Conditional routing based on business rules and validation checks
