**1. Leave Management Integration**
- **Data Synchronization**: Leave balances, accrual rates, and usage history sync between Time Off module in EC and ECP

- **Approval Workflow**: Time Off approvals in EC trigger corresponding payroll calculations in ECP

**2. Payroll Processing Integration**
- **Calculation Triggers**: Vacation payment requests initiate payroll operations in ECP
- **Payment Processing**: Summer vacation payments and lump sum calculations are processed through ECP
- **Result Synchronization**: Completed vacation payment results flow back to EC for record-keeping

**Full-Time Equivalent Processing**
- **FTE Fraction Calculation**: Employment information from EC provides FTE data to ECP for pro-rata calculations

**Enhanced Processing Logic**

**Data Mapping Requirements**
- Employee master data synchronization between EC and ECP

- Error handling and exception processing between modules

**Vacation Payment Lifecycle**

2. **Calculation**: ECP performs FTE-based pro-rata calculations using EC data
3. **Processing**: Payroll operations execute vacation payments
