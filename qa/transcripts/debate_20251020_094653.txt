FR-8	Essential	The solution must support the ongoing management of a hierarchical organisational structure across past, current and future organisational modelling scenarios.
FR-9	Essential	The solution must support the creation and ongoing management of the Department’s preferred job or workforce architecture framework consisting of job families, job functions, job roles and job titles.

FR-13	Essential	The solution must support the abolishing of jobs with a date effective.
FR-14	Essential	The solution must support the automatic inheriting of data attributes stored against jobs through to the creation of new positions.
FR-15	Essential	The solution must support the creation of positions with a date effective and a range of data attributes e.g. Position ID, Position Title, Locally Known As, Job Profile, Reports To, Supervisor Position, Number of Direct Reports, Budgeted FTE, Actual FTE, Number of Incumbents (Headcount), Vacant (Y/N – FTE), Position Type, Start Date, End Date, Funding Type, Organisational Unit, Location, Cost Centre, Award, Classification, Position Based Allowances (Site Specific Allowance), HR / Finance Delegations, Identified Position.
FR-16	Essential	The solution must support the loading and storage of role descriptions against positions.
FR-17	Essential	The solution must provide warnings when a request is submitted to create a new position would result in the approved FTE cap for that organisational unit to be exceeded.
FR-18	Essential	The solution must support the changing of positions with a date effective with the option to change / update any of the data attributes stored against the position.
FR-19	Essential	The solution must support the abolishing of positions with a date effective.
FR-20	Essential	The solution must support concurrent employment scenarios across the Department, including concurrent employment in the same award and concurrent employment across awards.
FR-21	Essential	The solution must support the bulk creation of positions to manage annual school allocations FTE and funding source with the option to split out to reflect school based direct and indirect reporting lines.
FR-22	Essential	The solution must support the bulk creation and update of organisational units and positions to manage Machinery of Government changes or large organisational restructures.
FR-23	Essential	The solution must allow line managers to create and edit positions based on allowable permissions (e.g. schools to create self-funded positions), which automatically updates the schools establishment without the need for additional workflow approvals.
FR-24	Essential	The solution must support Headcount and FTE management for Corporate positions at both a position level and positions within an organisational unit.
FR-25	Essential	The solution must support Resource and FTE management for School positions at both a position level and positions within an organisational unit.
FR-26	Essential	The solution must support a maximum allowed allocation of FTE against positions at multiple levels of the org structure (e.g. unit, branch or division).
FR-27	Essential	The solution must support vacancy management, including vacancy management reporting, across 1:1 position management, 1:Many position management and a hybrid model of both 1:1 and 1:Many establishment management model.
FR-28	Essential	The solution must support the workflow of create position and change position to internal teams (JADE – Job Analysis Design & Establishment, Finance) for review and or approval based on pre-defined parameters e.g. position is a permanent position or a Senior Executive Service (SES) position.
FR-29	Essential	The solution must support the creation and ongoing management of positions for volunteers, contingent / contractor employees and casuals in a talent pool via 1:Many position management.
FR-30	Essential	The solution must support automatic occupancy management from employee movements including concurrent employment.
FR-31	Essential	The solution must allow an employee to occupy multiple positions numbers concurrently and accurately reflect the occupied concurrent positions against the Department’s establishment management and organisational structure.
FR-32	Essential	The solution must support position based allowances that are automatically included in payroll calculations for any employee assigned to that position.
FR-33	Essential	The solution must support the automatic inheriting of data attributes stored against positions through to the creation of new hires, with the option to override at the employee level.
FR-34	Essential	The solution must support the creation of locations / centres with a date effective and a range of data attributes e.g. Location ID, Location Name, Location Address, Location Contact Details and Longitude and Latitude details.
FR-35	Essential	The solution must support the changing of locations / centres with a date effective with the option to change / update any of the data attributes stored against the location / centre.
FR-36	Essential	The solution must support the abolishing of locations / centres with a date effective.
FR-37	Essential	The solution must support the recording of an employee’s work site location and their physical location e.g. work site location is a remote location and their physical location for certain days of the week is a personal residence or virtual academy, which has pay outcome implications e.g. no entitlement location based allowances if working from a personal residence that is outside of the remote location.
FR-38	Essential	The solution must support organisation design reporting including vacancy management reporting, occupancy management reporting, headcount and FTE reporting, organisational structure reporting and establishment management reporting with a consolidated view across organisational units, jobs, positions, locations, concurrent employment and key employee information.

# Functional Specifications and Requirements Analysis: Manage Organisation Design

## Executive Summary

The Manage Organisation Design functional requirements represent a comprehensive organizational management capability within the SAP SuccessFactors HCMS solution. These requirements span organizational structure management, job architecture, position management, and establishment control with sophisticated multi-employment and concurrent employment support. The solution must integrate with existing systems while providing robust organizational modeling capabilities across past, present, and future states.

## Functional Specifications Analysis

### 1. Organizational Structure Management (FR-1 to FR-8)

#### Core Organizational Objects
- **Supported Entity Types**: Departments, divisions, regions, schools, centres, offices, branches, business units, jobs, positions, cost centres, and locations
- **Effective Dating**: All organizational changes must support effective dating for historical tracking and future planning
- **Data Attributes**: Standardized set including Org Unit ID, Title, Type, Status, Head Position ID, Cost Centre, Location, Transfer Rating

#### Integration Requirements
- **CIS Integration (FR-4)**: Real-time synchronization with Centre Information System for organizational unit data
- **Hierarchical Display**: Graphical and list format organizational charts with vacant/filled/shared position visibility
- **Multi-Scenario Support**: Historical, current, and future organizational modeling capabilities

### 2. Job Architecture Framework (FR-9 to FR-13)

#### Job Structure Management
- **Framework Support**: Department's preferred job/workforce architecture including job families, functions, roles, and titles
- **External Mappings**: ANZSCO code integration (e.g., 134311 – School Principal) for external reporting and compliance
- **Job Lifecycle**: Creation, modification, and abolishment with effective dating
- **Data Attributes**: Job ID, Family, Function, Role Levels 1&2, ANZSCO Code/Name, Certified Agreement

### 3. Position Management (FR-14 to FR-33)

#### Position Creation and Attributes
- **Automatic Inheritance (FR-14)**: New positions automatically inherit job data attributes
- **Comprehensive Position Data**: Position ID, Title, Locally Known As, Job Profile, Reporting Structure, FTE Management, Funding Types, Delegations, Allowances
- **Role Descriptions**: Storage and management of position-specific role descriptions
- **FTE Controls**: Automated warnings when FTE caps would be exceeded (FR-17)

#### Bulk Management Capabilities
- **Annual Allocations (FR-21)**: Bulk position creation for school FTE and funding management
- **Machinery of Government Changes (FR-22)**: Bulk organizational restructuring capabilities
- **Direct Management (FR-23)**: Line manager position creation with role-based permissions

#### Concurrent Employment Support
- **Multi-Award Employment (FR-20)**: Support for employment within same or different awards
- **Multiple Position Occupancy (FR-31)**: Employees can occupy multiple positions simultaneously with accurate establishment reflection
- **Automatic Occupancy Management (FR-30)**: Position occupancy updates based on employee movements

### 4. Location Management (FR-34 to FR-37)

#### Location Data Management
- **Geographic Information**: Location ID, Name, Address, Contact Details, Longitude/Latitude
- **Work Site Tracking**: Differentiation between work site location and physical residence for remote work scenarios
- **Pay Implications**: Location-based allowances and pay calculations

### 5. Reporting and Analytics (FR-38)

#### Consolidated Reporting Capabilities
- **Vacancy Management Reports**: Position vacancy tracking and analysis
- **Occupancy Management**: Position occupancy across multiple employment scenarios
- **Establishment Management**: Headcount and FTE reporting with organizational structure integration
- **Consolidated Views**: Cross-organizational unit reporting including concurrent employment and employee information

## Configuration vs Customization Analysis

### Standard SAP SuccessFactors Capabilities
- **Organizational Management**: Employee Central's built-in organizational structure management
- **Position Management**: Standard position creation, modification, and lifecycle management
- **Effective Dating**: Native support for organizational changes over time
- **Reporting**: Standard organizational reporting capabilities

### Customization Requirements
- **CIS Integration (FR-4)**: Custom interface for Centre Information System synchronization
- **Concurrent Employment (FR-20, FR-31)**: Enhanced position occupancy management beyond standard capabilities
- **Bulk Processing (FR-21, FR-22)**: Custom bulk upload interfaces for large-scale organizational changes
- **FTE Cap Warnings (FR-17)**: Custom validation logic for establishment controls
- **Location-Based Pay Calculations (FR-37)**: Integration with payroll for location-based allowances

## Integration Touchpoints

### External System Integrations
- **Centre Information System (CIS)**: Organizational unit data synchronization
- **Payroll System**: Position-based allowances and location-based pay calculations
- **Finance Systems**: Cost centre and funding type integration

### Internal SAP Components
- **Employee Central**: Core organizational and position data
- **Position Management**: Advanced position lifecycle management
- **Reporting**: Organizational analytics and establishment reporting

## Business Rules and Validation Criteria

### Establishment Controls
- **FTE Management**: Multi-level FTE allocation controls (unit, branch, division)
- **Cap Enforcement**: Automated warnings and controls for FTE limit compliance
- **Budget Validation**: Funding type and budget allocation verification

### Workflow Requirements
- **Approval Workflows**: Position creation/change routing to JADE, Finance teams
- **Parameter-Based Routing**: Workflow routing based on position type (permanent, SES)
- **Concurrent Employment Validation**: Multi-position assignment verification

## User Experience Requirements

### Manager Self-Service
- **Position Creation**: Line managers can create positions within defined permissions
- **Organizational Chart Access**: Real-time organizational structure visualization
- **Establishment Management**: FTE and headcount management capabilities

### Reporting Interface
- **Dashboard Views**: Organizational structure and establishment management dashboards
- **Multi-Format Display**: Graphical and list format organizational charts
- **Historical Comparison**: Past, current, and future state organizational views

## Testing Scenarios

### Organizational Structure Testing
- **Hierarchical Integrity**: Parent-child relationship validation
- **Effective Dating**: Historical and future organizational state verification
- **Bulk Processing**: Large-scale organizational change management

### Concurrent Employment Testing
- **Multi-Position Assignment**: Employee assignment to multiple positions
- **FTE Calculation**: Accurate FTE calculation across multiple positions
- **Pay Calculation**: Position-based allowance and location-based pay verification

### Integration Testing
- **CIS Synchronization**: Real-time data synchronization validation
- **Payroll Integration**: Position-based pay calculation accuracy
- **Finance Integration**: Cost centre and funding validation

## Risk Assessment and Mitigation

### High-Risk Areas
- **Concurrent Employment Complexity**: Multi-position assignment logic complexity
- **Integration Dependencies**: CIS and payroll system integration risks
- **Bulk Processing Performance**: Large-scale organizational change processing

### Mitigation Strategies
- **Phased Implementation**: Gradual rollout of concurrent employment features
- **Robust Testing**: Comprehensive integration and performance testing
- **Change Management**: Extensive user training for complex organizational management features

## Compliance and Security Considerations

### Data Privacy
- **Employee Information Protection**: Secure handling of concurrent employment data
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access to organizational management functions

### Audit Requirements
- **Change Tracking**: Comprehensive audit trail for organizational changes
- **Effective Dating Compliance**: Regulatory compliance for historical organizational data

This comprehensive functional specification provides the foundation for implementing sophisticated organizational management capabilities within the SAP SuccessFactors HCMS solution while maintaining alignment with standard SAP functionality and meeting the specific requirements of the Queensland Department of Education.
