# SAP SuccessFactors Metadata Extraction Report: Teacher & Teacher Aide Vacation Payments

## Executive Summary
This metadata extraction analyzes the vacation payment system for teachers and teacher aides within the Queensland Department of Education's SAP SuccessFactors HCM solution. The system encompasses complex eligibility rules, multiple payment types, and specialized processing requirements.

## 1. Core Object Definitions

### 1.1 Employee Central MDF Objects

**Summer Vacation Payment MDF Object**
- **Object Type**: MDF (Master Data Framework)
- **Purpose**: Store vacation payment calculations and projections
- **Self-Service Capability**: Yes (Employee preview functionality)
- **Key Attributes**: Payment type, hours, dates, eligibility status

**Teacher Vacation Payment Configuration**
- **Object Type**: Custom MDF Object
- **Access Level**: Employee Self-Service via homepage tile
- **Processing Period**: Available from October onwards for simulation
- **Integration**: Linked to ECP for calculation storage

### 1.2 Foundation Objects

**Employee Assignment (PerPerson)**
- **Related Objects**: Employment type, FTE calculations, leave balances
- **Key Fields**: Personnel Number, Assignment ID, Effective Dates
- **Relationships**: Multiple assignments support for teacher aides

**Payroll Result Objects**
- **Custom Payroll Operations**: Embedded within ECP processing
- **Retroactive Processing**: Standard SAP retro functionality
- **Performance Optimization**: Custom ECP tables for calculation storage

## 4. Business Rules Metadata

### 4.1 Teacher Eligibility Rules

**LSSP (Lump Sum Summer Payment)**
- Complete >100 days of service in school year
- Work entire Semester 2
- No breaks >9 days from permanent appointment

**LSOT (Lump Sum on Termination)**
- Additional 10 days service in Term 4
- 100 days of service in school year
- Applies when not eligible for Prorata or LSSP

**Prorata Calculation**
- Based on eligible service duration
- Adjusted for FTE changes throughout year
- Average FTE calculation methodology

### 4.2 Teacher Aide SJM Rules

**Standard Job Model (SJM)**
- 12.5% additional ordinary hours during term
- Deferred hours used for vacation payment calculation
- Easter/Winter/Spring vacation payment structure

**Multiple Assignment Processing**
- Average FTE calculation across assignments
- Combined engagement consideration
- Youth Detention Centre special handling
