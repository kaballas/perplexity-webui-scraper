Required Customization:
├── Custom MDF object: EmployeePositionAssignment

│   │   ├── employeeId (string): Employee identifier

│   │   └── primaryAssignment (boolean): Primary assignment indicator

│   └── Associations: employeeId, positionId
├── Modified JobInfo schema: Support multiple assignments per employee

└── Custom payroll integration: Split FTE calculations for payroll processing
