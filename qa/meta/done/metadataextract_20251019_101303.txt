# SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding 2H 2025 Metadata Extraction Report

## Executive Summary
This comprehensive metadata extraction analyzes critical onboarding issues identified in the 2H 2025 Preview Release, focusing on Custom MDF objects, e-signature workflows, and candidate initiation processes. The analysis covers system configurations, object dependencies, and permission frameworks affecting the Queensland Department of Education HCMS implementation.
